Event-1 Ambition

It all started on 5th January 2014 when we went to SHIVANANDA ASHRAM and taught them the basic important traffic rules and also tested their skills and rewarded them accordingly.

Event-2 Ambition

Then for the second event , we went to UDHAVUM ULLANGAL ILLAM ashram(Gokulapuram branch) where we taught the children about the organs of senses and a few educational games were conducted and awards were given.

Event-3 Ambition

The main concentration of this event was to analyze and develop the computing skills of young aspiring children of UDHAVUM ULLANGAL ILLAM ashram (St Thomas Mount).

Event-4 Ambition

Computer classes were organized for four days to develop the the overall skill set of the children. Microsoft office was introduced to the children and they were taught to use the internet services for their benefits. Event took place in SHIVANANDA ASHRAM.

Event-5 Ambition

Painting competition was organized for refreshment and recognizing the talent of the youth. Handprints were taken of every child and prizes were given to the winners. Again the event held at SHIVANANDA ASHRAM.

Event-6 Ambition

The importance of hygiene was explained to the children and the basic habits were taught to them at CHILD CARE FOUNDATION.

Event-7 Ambition

The children were educated about the basic rights and other moral values so as to help them boost up their personalities

Event-8 Ambition

Various events comprising of quizzes and educational sessions were held at ULLANGAL ILLAM ashram (Gokulapuram branch).

Event-9 Ambition

On this event, the children were inspired by means of some motivational videos and later a drawing competition and few other ball games were arranged and thereby making their day.

The Street Store

The 'Blooming Beacon' comes up with the first-of its-kind street shopping experience in collaboration with a South-African organisation "The Street Store" Has it ever struck you that even the routine shopping you do, could be an experience of a lifetime for many? With a vision to make a difference in the lives of the underprivileged, the 'Blooming Beacon' comes up with the first-of its-kind street shopping experience in collaboration with a South-African organisation "The Street Store". Extend a helping hand to this noble cause, with your contribution of clothes and accessories.

Blooming Beacon Branches

This is an organization which was created to help poor people of our nation and to Empower Women. It has 2 Branches in India , one in CHENNAI and the other in SIWAN . The main Ambition of this organization is to make sure that the poor children of our nation get full Education and stay away from child labour and live life as normal people. We will be very Thankful to you if you will be a part of our organization and help poor children to Educate them
